Important Information

CTCOVID.com allows you to track cases of COVID-19 in Connecticut. You can track case, death and hospitalization statistics on a daily basis for your city, town, county or statewide.

This website does not provide medical or public health advice and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. Please visit the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention website for more information about COVID-19.

This website is operated by a private individual and is in no way connected, affiliated, associated or operated by any governmental agency, university, medical organization or any public or private health organization.

COVID-19 case data is updated Monday through Friday by 5:30pm EST (barring any delays from the State in providing data) using data provided by Connecticut Open Data, CovidActNow and Rt Live. Vaccination data is updated daily by 8:00PM EST (barring any delays from the CDC in providing data) using data provided by Centers for Disease Control Vaccination Tracker.
